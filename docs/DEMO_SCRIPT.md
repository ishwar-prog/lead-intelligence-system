# 2-Minute Demo Script

**0:00-0:15 — Landing page**
Show the gauge sweep animation on load. One sentence: "An AI lead
intelligence system — scores, drafts outreach, and keeps a human in
the loop at every step."

**0:15-0:35 — Register/Login**
Show creating an account briefly. One sentence: "Every account only
ever sees its own leads — enforced at the database layer, not just
hidden in the UI."

**0:35-1:00 — Paste & Extract**
Paste a sample lead description live. Show fields populating, and
specifically point out a field that comes back empty/null: "Notice
it didn't guess the budget — it told me that wasn't in the text."

**1:00-1:30 — Submit and watch it resolve**
Submit the lead. Show the card appear as "Analyzing…", then update
automatically to a scored result with the gauge animating in - no
manual refresh.

**1:30-1:50 — Detail panel + human review**
Open the lead, show the full AI output (score reasoning, drafted
email, responsible-AI caution), then submit a human review.

**1:50-2:00 — Close on the Trust page**
Quick cut to `/trust` - "Every claim here maps to an actual design
decision in the code, not a disclaimer."
